Project 01

SharePoint Modernization

From paper-based chaos to digital collaboration

The Challenge

Teams relied on paper forms, email chains, and local file storage. No version control, difficult to find documents, and impossible to track workflow progress.

The Solution

Created standardized SharePoint sites with integrated workflows, document libraries with version control, and custom forms for common processes. Built templates that other teams could replicate.

  • Automated approval workflows reducing turnaround time
  • Centralized document management with full history
  • Custom forms replacing 15+ paper-based processes
  • Mobile access for field workers
  • Integration with Power BI for real-time reporting

The Solution

  • Automated deficiency tracking with email alerts

  • Real-time compliance dashboards for management
  • Comprehensive training program ensuring adoption

Project 03

Signalling Incident Control System (SINCS)

Transforming safety-critical telecoms failure management

The Challenge

Three-year backlog of Safety Related Telecoms Failures (SRTFs) with no standardized process for tracking, managing, or learning from incidents. New engineers had no formal training framework.

The Solution

Redesigned the entire SINCS workflow, creating clear accountability chains and resolution processes aligned with ISO 45001 requirements. Developed comprehensive training materials that became the standard onboarding framework.

Project 07

Predictive Site Analytics

Identifying problems before they become failures

The Challenge

Reactive maintenance approach meant addressing problems only after failures occurred. No systematic way to identify high-risk sites or predict potential issues.

The Solution

Used Power Query to analyze historical failure data, maintenance patterns, equipment age, and environmental factors. Developed risk scoring model that identified 61 problem sites across eastern England requiring targeted attention.
